How I Work

Three engagement models, each designed around what the work actually requires.

Fractional CPO

2–3 days/week · 3–6 month minimum

Embedded part-time product leadership for organizations that have a product function but no senior head of product. I own the roadmap, drive prioritization, align stakeholders, and coach your team. The work compounds — that's why there's a minimum engagement length.

Best for: Startups and growing companies that need a CPO-level operator without a full-time salary commitment, or organizations managing a leadership gap while they search for a permanent hire.

Product Advisor

Project-based or monthly retainer

Strategic guidance without day-to-day involvement. Useful when you have a capable product lead who needs a senior sounding board — for roadmap reviews, prioritization frameworks, or navigating a specific challenge that requires experience to untangle.

Best for: Founders, CPOs, and heads of product who want an experienced thinking partner without adding headcount. Also useful for teams facing a discrete strategic challenge — a platform decision, a market shift, a reorg — where an outside perspective adds leverage.

AI Integration Advisory

Typically project-based

Helping product teams identify where AI and LLM tools actually fit into their workflows — and where they don't. I built and ran an AI enablement program for 20+ product teams at Chewy. I know what's practical, what's hype, and how to help teams ship AI-powered features without burning six months on the wrong approach.

Best for: Product organizations trying to build an AI strategy, teams building AI-powered features for the first time, or leaders trying to understand what the current tooling landscape actually means for their roadmap and team structure.

What You Get

Concrete deliverables and outcomes, each grounded in real work.

  • Product strategy and roadmap development. Not a framework for its own sake. A clear direction built from customer research and business constraints, with a prioritization approach your team can use six months after I'm gone.

  • Quantitative prioritization frameworks. Built and implemented a prioritization system at Chewy that eliminated peak-season escalation cycles across 20+ product teams. Good prioritization is repeatable — it shouldn't require a senior PM to make every call.

  • Data infrastructure and analytics strategy. Re-architected Amazon's operational metrics platform serving 30,000 daily users across 300+ fulfillment centers, cutting load times from 3 minutes to 5 seconds. If your team can't measure what matters, that's where the work starts.

  • Team coaching and performance management. Managed organizations of 13+ direct reports across multiple sites. Coached 20% to promotion; turned around 30% of underperformers through direct feedback and structured development plans. The goal is a team that doesn't need me in the room to make good decisions.

  • Compliance and GRC product strategy. Built Chewy's first automated compliance analytics platform from scratch — re-architecting instrumentation across all product surfaces into a unified metrics framework — and reduced audit cycles by 50%. Compliance work doesn't have to be a grind. If it's built right, it becomes an operational advantage.

  • AI/LLM workflow integration for product teams. Designed and ran Chewy's AI enablement program, equipping 20+ product teams with practical LLM workflows and eliminating 20 hours per week in analyst overhead. The goal is never AI for its own sake — it's sustainable productivity gains that hold up once the engagement ends.
